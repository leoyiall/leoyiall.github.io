Zhang Yiyun not in the traditional sense of the good student, he grew up playing the game at 13 years old started learning programming, then write the a Trojan horse program, using the Trojans earn the life first bucket of gold. Monthly income of about seven thousand or eight thousand yuan.

With the popularity of anti-virus software, he no longer do Trojans, but in high school with a few friends to do a online hacker training base, the monthly income has reached more than ten thousand yuan.

"I'm a friend of those who are different, and I'm more concerned about how technology is realized." Zhang Yiyun business sense is very keen. However, Zhang Yiyun believes that these are minor, the real business or starting from last year. He and Guangzhou University huaruan college with several classmates together to do a "pocket part-time a PP, he believes, the mobile terminal market of part-time job is a piece of" blue ocean ".

"Product development, the original set of investors suddenly do not vote, I almost have to declare the company bankrupt." Can be the same circle of friends suddenly gave him an investor, we talked about two hours, and then the investment side agreed to invest 2000000."

Even the staff salary is out of the ordinary "bursts". Inside the company to set up a pocket gold, each employee has three gold coins every day, corresponding to the relative amount of cash. When work, the difference between the staff vote, gold coins up to three, can be converted into a gold coin. "The purpose of this is to go to the center.
